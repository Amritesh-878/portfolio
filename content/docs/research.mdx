## Paper: Reinforcement Learning for Constraint Satisfaction Game Agents

Amritesh Praveen and Gagan Venkat, May 2026.
DOI: [10.5281/zenodo.20076630](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.20076630).

The paper works up from tabular reinforcement-learning methods, through deep Q-networks, to
an adversarial PPO-trained Wumpus agent that learns to hunt using memory-based tracking
rather than direct sight. The system it describes is the game you can
[play right here](/projects/hunter-wumpus/play), or, as the paper puts it, just play the
paper.

## Patent application: Railway Track Monitoring and Repairing Device

Indian patent application **202541017469 A**, published 7 March 2025. A track-riding trolley
with acoustic defect sensors, a robotic repair link with a motorized cutter, GPS, an EMI
sensor, and an AI-based imaging unit that monitors gravel distribution.
